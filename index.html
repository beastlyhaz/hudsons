About us


Hudsons at Mueller is conviently located on Level 2 of MPAC at Mueller College, Rothwell and is open from 8am - 3pm school days. We pride outselves on our freshly ground coffee and our assortment of declicious mains and desserts. Why not come in and try today's bake of cakes and muffins in the cabinent.


Here at Hudsons we proudly offer a 100% satisfaction guarentee. If you have any questions, complaints or concerns, feel free to contact us between 8am to 4pm, Monday to Friday.
