    THE PASS CAFE

Byron'sfavourite Cafe

The Pass Cafe Byron Bay is a fully licensed venue overlooking the iconic Pass Beach and surf break.

We are open 7 days, serving breakfast from 7am until 11:30 am and lunch from 12pm until 2:30pm

Our menu includes local favorites, gluten free options, vegetarian and vegan, available to takeaway or enjoy in our outdoor dining area or overlooking the view from our beautiful covered deck. We serve you everything from yummy breakfast bowls and pancakes, to satisfying lunches like our tasty salads and burgers. Cakes and desserts are available as well. See you soon…
